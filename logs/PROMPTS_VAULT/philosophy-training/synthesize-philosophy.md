## ROLE

## TASK
Extract unique methodological insights and present them as a numbered set of key principles, each with a short explanation and actionable rule.

1. Principle Name — 1–2 sentence explanation; Action: one concrete rule.
2. ...
3. ...

### Practices
- Drill/Practice — frequency — metric for success

### Summary
- 3–4 sentences connecting principles to day-to-day training decisions.
